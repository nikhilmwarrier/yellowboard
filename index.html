Welcome to YellowBoard!

Here you can type stuff, and it will be auto saved [using localStorage() API.]
You can use YellowBoard as a quick notepad. Everything you type here will be autosaved, and can be reatained even after you close your browser.

Experimental: Markdown Support

YellowBoard supports markdown, thanks to MarkedJS, but it has some issues.
If you want to preview your markdown, you need to referesh your browser. Also, you can write only one line of markdown-formatted text, after which, you have to refresh the page. Else, the formatting will not be applied.
